The MK54 lightweight torpedo's planar sonar array is the active/passive guidance section that homes the weapon onto a maneuvering submarine target — the primary ASW torpedo in service with U.S. and allied navies. L3Harris produces the sonar array at its Salt Lake City facility. As Chief Engineer I led the engineering team responsible for sonar-section production, depot-level testing, and obsolescence management across the fielded inventory.
