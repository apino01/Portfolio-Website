A 60+ kW-class shipboard laser weapon system integrated into the Aegis combat system aboard Arleigh Burke-class destroyers — the first tactical directed-energy weapon delivered to the operational U.S. fleet. Working within Lockheed Martin's Advanced Technical Leadership Program at the Baltimore facility, I supported hardware development and system integration through design review, driving the power and thermal subsystems from early development toward fleet delivery.
